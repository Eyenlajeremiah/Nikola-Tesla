Dr. Nikola Tesla

The man who invented electricity

Here's a time line of Necolas Tesla life

  • Born - 10 July 1856 Smiljan, Austrian Empire
  • Died - 7 January 1943 (aged 86) New York City, U.S.
  • Resting Place - Nikola Tesla Museum, Belgrade, Serbia
  • Citizenship - Austria (until 1891) U.S. (from 1891)
  • Education - Graz University of Technology (dropped out)
  • Occupations - Engineer - futurist - inventor
  • Awards - 1. Order of St. Sava (1892), 2. Elliott Cresson Medal (1894), 3. Order of Prince Danilo I (1895), 4. AIEE Edison Medal (1916), 5. Order of the Yugoslav Crown (1931), 6. John Scott Medal (1934), 7. Order of the White Eagle (1937), 8. Order of the White Lion (1937)
